Yule Fuel

ABV: 6.3%

Brewer: Joe Hannis

Style: ESB

This is my idea of a hybrid Christmas beer. I wanted to make a British ESB, but use some of the techniques and additions that you would usually find in a Belgian Dubbel to create a great Christmas Beer.

It's Maris Otter and DarkCrystal, and I used First Gold Hops. All very ESB, but with a step mash. With 10 minutes left in the boil, I added cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and bitter orange peel. I made a mistake and forgot to add molasses sugar to the boil, so decided to risk it and put it in the fermenter, considerably upping the ABV. I fermented hot to deliberately bring out the fruity esters.

The beer is a winter warmer, with a spicy, dark fruits aroma and a dark, sticky molasses and caramel flavour.
