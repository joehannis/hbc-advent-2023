Breakfast At Flahertys

ABV: 9.7%

Brewer: Joe Hannis

Style: Imperial Stout

My ode to the Irish coffee in a big stout. A dry Irish stout recipe thats been beefed up to get a high ABV, I also added a hefty amount of oats to get a thick creaminess (as it is well known, lactose in beer is an abomination). After getting an ABV of 7.9%, I added a litre of Jameson and 'dry beaned' for 48hrs with 150g of coffee to get to my 9.7% ABV.

It's big on dark chocolate, coffee and vanilla flavours with a big punch at the end from the whiskey. One for sipping at room temp.
